Privacy Policy

Welcome to NeighborHub ("we", "us", "our"), a community platform designed to connect neighbors and share information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: When you sign in via Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Data: We collect aggregated analytics such as pages visited and interactions to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and personalize the experience.
  • To store and display posts, events, and other community content you create.
  • To respond to inquiries or communications sent to our contact email.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with third-party services (e.g., Firebase) that assist with hosting, analytics, and authentication under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We follow industry-standard measures to protect data. However, no transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You can delete your account by contacting us at quantaforgeai@gmail.com. You may also adjust settings in your Google account related to permissions.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please email us at quantaforgeai@gmail.com.
